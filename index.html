Our Mission

A world where wildlife
thrives alongside us.

WildHeart exists to bridge the gap between human development and animal welfare — through rescue, advocacy, education, and habitat restoration.

🐾
Rescue & Rehabilitation
24/7 emergency response teams operating in 92 countries to rescue injured and endangered animals.
🌍
Habitat Restoration
Restoring natural ecosystems and creating safe corridors for wildlife migration and breeding.
🔬
Research & Advocacy
Science-led policy work pushing for stronger wildlife protection laws internationally.

Our Journey

15 years of impact.

2009
WildHeart Founded

Three veterinarians and a wildlife biologist launch WildHeart from a converted barn in Montana, rescuing their first 12 animals.

2013
First International Sanctuary

Opened our first overseas facility in Kenya, becoming the first US charity to operate an elephant sanctuary on African soil.

2016
UN Partnership Formed

Officially recognized as an NGO partner by the UNEP, bringing WildHeart into global conservation policy discussions.

2020
10,000 Animals Milestone

Celebrated rescuing our 10,000th animal — a snow leopard named Frost, found injured in Nepal.

2024
Ocean Guardians Launch

Launched our marine division with a fleet of 8 monitoring vessels, expanding our reach to protect ocean biodiversity.
